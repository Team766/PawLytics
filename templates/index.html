Welcome to PawLytics, the home of FRC statistics and analytics!
PawLytics is a web application that provides a comprehensive platform through student-collected scouting data for analyzing and understanding FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) robot preformance.
Whether you're a student, coach, strategist, mentor, or fan, PawLytics offers a wide range of tools and resources to help you make sense of FRC data.
